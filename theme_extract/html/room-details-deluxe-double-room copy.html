Deluxe Double Room with Extra Bed

from €140 /Night

The Deluxe Double Room with Extra Bed at Hotel Inova offers an exceptional blend of spacious comfort and elegant design, measuring 27 m² and featuring both garden and city views. The room is equipped with a range of modern amenities to ensure a relaxing stay, including air-conditioning, soundproofing, a flat-screen TV with cable and satellite channels, free WiFi, and an extra-large double bed noted for its comfort by guests.

Guests can enjoy a private kitchenette with a refrigerator, coffee machine, electric kettle, and all necessary facilities to prepare and store food. The private bathroom includes complimentary toiletries, a shower or bath, towels, slippers, a hairdryer, and toilet paper for added convenience. Additional touches such as a minibar, tea/coffee maker, and a dressing room further enhance the sense of luxury and practicality.

The room also boasts a patio, desk, seating area, wardrobe or closet, laptop safe, ironing facilities, air purifiers, and smoke/carbon monoxide detectors for safety. Located in a private apartment section of the building, the Deluxe Double Room is accessible by elevator and maintains an atmosphere of peace with single-room air conditioning, soundproof walls, and a strict no-smoking policy.
